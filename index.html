More About Me

Howdy!

Hi, I am Sonal Dalvi, an aspiring student from Amity University. I’m doing my bachelors in Fashion Design. I am someone who has loved fashion from the core of my heart, hence I always wanted to pursue this field. This blog is about upcycling old clothes into a new, fashionable outfit.

In other words, we can even say it’s all about giving your old clothes a second life. Usually, we tend to rant that we don’t have anything to wear, but we always miss out on the valuable treasure we have in our wardrobes. Sometimes we could convert the old saree into a dress or a skirt to a top and the list of possibilities is endless. By doing this you can and you will save your money and yet enjoy the pleasure of wearing something old yet new. On the other hand, there is a bigger purpose associated with upcycling of the clothes, as it helps to protect the environment. When the clothes are thrown away, they take a lot of time to degrade. This leads to massive pollution. Thus, treating the clothes and reusing them for yourself, increases the life of the clothes and saves them from just been a reason for harmful gases in the air. After all, it’s said right old is gold. So, don’t let the treasure in your wardrobe end up in scrap.
